>> Online Retail UK – Sales & Customer Analytics (Power BI)
This project analyzes transactional data from a UK-based online retail company (Dec 2010 – Dec 2011). The goal is to understand sales trends, customer behavior, retention, and lifetime value using Power BI.

>> Objectives
Analyze sales performance and seasonality
Segment customers based on lifetime revenue
Measure monthly retention and churn
Perform cohort retention analysis
Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

>>Tools & Skills Used
Power BI
DAX (CALCULATE, Time Intelligence, Cohort Logic)
Data Modeling (Star Schema)
Customer Segmentation
Retention & Churn Analysis

>> Key Insights
Total sales reached £991.7M during the period
Strong Q4 seasonal spike
High-value customers contribute disproportionately to revenue
Average monthly retention rate ~37%
Average customer lifetime ~13 months

>> Data Notes
Data covers December 2010 – December 2011 only
No demographic data available
Canceled transactions removed
